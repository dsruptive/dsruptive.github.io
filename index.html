Electronics without batteries

We are researching in flexible circuit boards. The power is then stored within the microchip without the use of batteries and can for example be used to read sensors within the body.

Implantable Electronics

Our Board has a NFC transponder that can be inserted into the body with a syringe. The transponder can replace your keys, pin-codes, member cards and passage cards or key fobs. It has the potential to replace tickets for commuting, credit cards and even identification.

Temporary Tattoos

Simply attach the conductive tattoo like an adhesive patch and use your mobile phone to instantly read the body temperature or for temporary transport or identification method.
